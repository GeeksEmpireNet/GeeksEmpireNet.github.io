👩‍🏫 Instruction Of Floating Shortcuts/Folders 🔵 🟠 ⚫ 🟡 ⚪ 🔴
 
1️⃣  Install & Open the App
2️⃣  Now You See List of Your Installed Applications & Games
3️⃣  Click on an App/Game to Create a Floating Shortcuts
 
⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇
 
👨‍🏫 Options Of Floating Shortcuts/Folders 🔵 🟠 ⚫ 🟡 ⚪ 🔴
 
1️⃣  Press & Hold on Floating Shortcuts
2️⃣  Split It | Directly Open App in Split Window (Multi-Window)
3️⃣  Float It | Open App in Floating Window Like Desktop Operating System (Extra Steps Required which Explained Inside the App)
4️⃣  Pin Shortcut | Pin Floating Shortcut in the Position to Avoid Accidental Movement
5️⃣  Remove Shortcut | Self Explanatory 😉
👽 Extra Features of Floating Shortcuts/Folders 🔵 🟠 ⚫ 🟡 ⚪ 🔴
 
✨ Lock It 🔒 | Open Apps By Your Fingerprint & Pin Password
▫️ Synchronize Your Locked Apps/Folders/Widgets List
▫️ Press & Hold on Apps/Folders/Widgets in the List & Select 'Lock It 🔒'
✨ Floating Notification 🔘 
▫️ Click on Notification Dot to See Contents
▫️ Press & Hold on Notification Dot to Expand Notification Panel
▫️ Swipe Left/Right to Remove Notifications
▫️ Turn On Floating Notifications in Preferences ⚙
✨ Sticky Edge
▫️ Open Notification Bar & Select Move to Edge (Left/Right) to Move Floating Shortcuts/Folders to Edge
 
 
🌟 To Move & Shoot Floating Shortcuts/Folders Around Easier, Go to Preferences ⚙ & Change Fling Sensitivity 🌟
ℹ Introducing Floating Widgets 🔳 🔲
 
Floating Widgets Are the Best Way to Access your Data Quickly from Multiple Widgets Simultaneously without Compromising your Data Privacy & Integrity.
🌟 Benefits Of Using Floating Widgets...
✨ It Helps To Avoid Battery Draining 🔋
✨ It Helps To Have Clean Home Screen 😍
✨ It Helps To Secure Your Widgets Data & Increase Your Privacy 🔏
(ℹ Press & Hold on Configured Widgets to Create Home Screen Shortcuts ℹ)
👩‍🏫 Instruction Of Floating Widgets 🔳 🔲
 
1️⃣  Click on 'Floating Widgets' Button
2️⃣  Click on Pulsating (+) Button to See List Of Installed Widgets
3️⃣  Press & Hold on an Installed Widget
4️⃣  Configure the Selected Widget (If There is an Option)
5️⃣  Grant Widgets Permission After Seeing this Text "Create widget and allow access?"
6️⃣  Click on '✔' to Open the Configured Widgets in Floating Window
 
⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇⬇
 
👨‍🏫 Options Of Floating Widgets 🔳 🔲
 
1️⃣  Press & Hold on Configured Widgets to See the Options
2️⃣  Create Home Screen Shortcut | Create A Shortcut Of Floating Widget on Home to Open the Widget Directly from Home Screen in Floating Window
3️⃣  Add To Recovery | Add the Selected Floating Widgets to Recreate It Remotely
